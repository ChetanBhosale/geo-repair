# How to get recommended by ChatGPT and Perplexity

> AI recommendations depend on clear entity signals, third-party proof, and category sources. Your homepage is only one part of the recommendation system.

**June 3, 2026** · AI Recommendations, Entity Trust, Reddit Questions · By GEO Repair

When someone asks ChatGPT or Perplexity for a business recommendation, the answer rarely comes only from the business homepage. AI systems look for category consensus: review pages, directories, comparison posts, forum discussions, publications, and clear entity signals.

Your site still matters, but it is only one part of the recommendation system.

## Why do competitors get recommended?

Competitors usually show up because the web repeatedly connects them to the category. That connection may come from:

- Roundup posts
- Review sites
- Marketplace listings
- Reddit and forum discussions
- YouTube reviews
- Industry publications
- Comparison pages
- Local profiles
- Their own clear site structure

The AI system is not simply asking, "Who has the best homepage?" It is asking, "Which names appear trustworthy for this category?"

## What should you check first?

Run three prompts:

1. "What is [your brand]?"
2. "Best [category] tools or providers for [buyer type]"
3. "[Your brand] alternatives"

Then inspect the sources behind the answer when the engine shows them. If competitors appear and you do not, write down the cited URLs. That list is more useful than generic advice.

## What can you control on your own site?

Make your entity clear:

- Say what you do in one plain sentence
- Use consistent category language across pages
- Add Organization and WebSite structured data
- Build comparison, use case, pricing, and security pages when relevant
- Make important content visible in server-rendered HTML
- Answer buyer-intent questions directly

This helps the system understand you, but it may not be enough to recommend you.

## What can you control off-site?

Find the sources AI systems already use for your category. Then work toward legitimate presence there: customer reviews, directories, partner pages, industry lists, community discussion, and editorial coverage.

There is no shortcut that guarantees recommendations. The realistic path is entity clarity on your own site plus proof in the places AI systems already trust.
